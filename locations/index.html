Services we provide

Locations We Serve

Strategically positioned across Oman’s key ports and free zones, Sohar Mirrors Trading ensures seamless marine and trade support to vessels calling at Khasab, Sohar, Duqm Fishing Port, Duqm and Salalah.
Your gateway to efficient, reliable, and region-wide supply chain solutions.

Ports & locations served

Port of Khasab

Located in the Musandam Peninsula, bordering the UAE, Khasab serves international shipping companies, cruise liners, regional ferries, dhows, and local distributors, operating near the Strait of Hormuz. Passengers in central tourist areas and companies, enjoy our range of products and services.

Port & Freezone of Sohar

A deep-sea port centrally located, situated close to the UAE, within easy reach of the booming economies of the Gulf and the Indian subcontinent.

Sohar is a major commercial and industrial port capable of handling the world`s largest ships, handling Cargo, Petroleum products, Minerals, Metals, as well as container shipping

The surrounding free zone, is a key location for our ship, marine services, and industrial supply division.

Duqm Fishing Port

One of the largest multi-purpose fishing ports in the region, focused on commercial fishing and seafood processing with refrigerated facilities.

Our supply division services and supports the local fishing sector and maritime supply chain.

 

 

Port & Freezone of Duqm

Duqm is a fast-growing industrial hub on the central coast. It houses a deep-sea port, a dry dock for ship repair, and large-scale logistics infrastructure.

The surrounding free zone, is the perfect key location for our ship, marine services, and industrial supply division.

 

Port & Freezone of Salalah

Located in the southern Dhofar region. Due to its geographical location & duty-free status, Salalah is a key gateway to Aden, North Eastern Africa and Western Asia.

The port handles container, general & bulk cargo, and offers duty-free trading within the free zone. Our customers, enjoy our range of products and services, offered by our supply division
